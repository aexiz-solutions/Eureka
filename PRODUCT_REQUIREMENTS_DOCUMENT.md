# 📘 EUREKA – PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## 1. 🧠 Product Overview
Eureka is an AI-powered retail store intelligence platform that enables users to design, simulate, optimize, and operate entire retail environments—from store-level zoning to shelf-level product placement—using real-world data, computer vision, and intelligent automation.

> **Core idea:** A closed-loop system that connects Design → Execution → Measurement → Optimization → Scale.

---

## 2. 🎯 Goals & Objectives
- Provide an intuitive canvas for full store + shelf design
- Enable AI-assisted merchandising and layout optimization
- Connect layouts to real-world execution and performance
- Support multi-store rollout and experimentation
- Improve sales, space efficiency, and compliance

---

## 3. 👥 Target Users
- Merchandisers
- Category Managers
- Retail HQ Teams
- Store Managers
- Auditors
- Suppliers (future)

## 4. 🧩 Feature Requirements (Detailed)

---

### 4.1 🎨 Drag & Drop + AI Layout Builder
**Description:** Visual canvas to design store layout and shelves.

**Capabilities:**
- Drag & drop shelves, zones, products
- Auto snap grid & alignment
- Templates (store types)
- AI-assisted layout suggestions

**Value:** Fast, intuitive design (Canva/Figma-like)

---

### 4.2 📦 Product Placement (Size + Category)
**Description:** Intelligent placement based on physical and semantic properties.

**Capabilities:**
- Size-aware placement (height, width, depth)
- Category grouping
- Brand blocking
- Facing calculation

**Value:** Realistic + optimized layouts

---

### 4.3 🤖 Smart Shelf Builder (AI Optimization)
**Description:** Self-optimizing shelf system.

**Inputs:**
- Sales data
- Product category
- Customer behavior

**Rules:**
- Best-selling items at eye level
- High-demand → more facings
- Category clustering

**Output:**
- Optimized shelf layouts
- Real-time suggestions

---

### 4.4 📊 Analytics (Sales & Efficiency)
**Description:** Measure performance of layouts.

**Metrics:**
- Sales per product/shelf
- Revenue per sq ft
- SKU performance

**Value:** Data-driven decisions

---

### 4.5 📈 Real-Time Analytics Dashboard
**Description:** Live visualization of performance.

**Features:**
- Layout performance score
- Visual insights
- Drill-down views

---

### 4.6 💰 Sales per Shelf Section
- Revenue per zone
- Identify high-performing areas

---

### 4.7 🟥 Dead Zone Detection
- Detect low-performing areas
- Suggest improvements

---

### 4.8 📏 Space Utilization
- % of used vs unused space
- Overcrowding detection

---

### 4.9 📸 Image-Based Shelf Recognition AI
**Description:** Validate real-world execution.

**Capabilities:**
- Product detection
- Planogram matching
- Missing/incorrect placement detection

---

### 4.10 🔥 Heatmap Visualization

#### Footfall Heatmap
- Customer movement tracking
- High/low traffic zones

#### Shelf Interaction Heatmap
- Product engagement
- Visibility vs interaction

---

### 4.11 🔔 Auto Replenishment Alerts
**Description:** Predict stockouts.

**Capabilities:**
- Time-to-stockout prediction
- Priority alerts
- Shelf-level monitoring

---

### 4.12 👥 Multi-User Collaboration
- Real-time editing
- Role-based access (RBAC)
- Comments & approvals

---

### 4.13 🏢 Multi-Store Sync
- Centralized layout management
- Store-specific adaptation
- Real-time updates

---

### 4.14 🧪 A/B Testing (Store + Shelf)
- Layout experimentation
- Performance comparison
- Auto rollout of winner

## 6. 🔄 End-to-End User Flow

1. Define store parameters
2. Design layout (manual + AI)
3. Place shelves & products
4. AI optimization
5. Deploy to stores
6. Store execution
7. Capture images + sales data
8. Analyze via CV + analytics
9. Generate insights
10. Optimize layout
11. Repeat loop
